About

What This Demo Is

The full Vibe Check stack is two pieces:

  • Backend — a FastAPI service with scheduled scrapers (HN, Reddit), an LLM scoring pipeline, and a SQLite-backed snapshot store. Exposes /api/v1/digest/latest and friends.
  • Frontend — a standalone Vite app that renders the digests as a dashboard.

What you're seeing here is a simplified, infra-free version of the frontend: it skips the backend entirely and pulls one live source the browser can reach (the Hacker News public API), plus a hand-curated LLM leaderboard.

Full app: backend ingests → LLMs analyze → API serves → frontend renders. This page does step 1 and step 4 only.
